Who We Are

One studio. A portfolio of ventures.

Black Star Media & Entertainment is an independent media management company headquartered in Brisbane, Queensland. We create, produce and operate a growing family of brands — from AI-composed music and original YouTube channels to hands-on AI education.

Everything we make is united by one idea: that technology, when paired with genuine craft, can bring people together and tell better stories. We treat AI as a creative engine, not a gimmick — a way to make music, media and education that wasn't possible yesterday.
