Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

1. Who we are

Black Star Media & Entertainment ("Black Star Media", "we", "us" or "our") is an independent media company based in Brisbane, Queensland, Australia, operating the website blackstarmedia.com.au (the "Site"). This policy explains what personal information we collect from visitors, how we use it, and the choices you have. It is written to comply with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), and to give European visitors the information required under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at contact@blackstarmedia.com.au.

2. Information we collect

Information you give us directly

  • Contact form: when you submit the form on our Contact section, we collect your name, email address, subject and message.
  • Free resource / newsletter sign-up: when you request one of our free PDF guides, we collect your email address, log it to a secure Google Sheet, and send you the resource by automated email. You may unsubscribe at any time using the link included in that email or by contacting us directly.
  • Direct email: if you email us at contact@blackstarmedia.com.au, we receive whatever information you choose to include.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we (and the third-party services we use, described below) automatically collect some technical information when you visit, including your IP address, browser and device type, pages viewed, referring page, and the date and time of your visit. This is collected via cookies and similar technologies (such as local storage and web beacons) placed by us and by our third-party service providers.

3. Cookies & advertising (Google AdSense)

This Site displays advertising served by Google through Google AdSense. Google and its partners use cookies — including the DoubleClick cookie — to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. Third-party vendors, including Google, may also use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site or other sites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the internet. You can learn more about how Google uses information from sites that use its services at policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites.

You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices. Most browsers also let you block or delete cookies through their settings.

4. Other third-party services we use

We use a small number of trusted third-party services to run the Site. Each has its own privacy policy governing how it handles data:

  • Google AdSense — serves the advertising on this Site.
  • Google Fonts — loads our typefaces; your browser connects directly to Google's font servers.
  • YouTube (privacy-enhanced mode) — video embeds are loaded on click via youtube-nocookie.com, which limits cookie use until you choose to play a video.
  • Spotify — powers the embedded music player on our Music section.
  • Google Drive & Google Apps Script/Sheets — host our downloadable PDF resources and process the email sign-up form for them.
  • Formspree — processes submissions from our Contact form and forwards them to our inbox.

5. How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries submitted through our contact form or by email.
  • To deliver the free resources you request and, where you've opted in, to send occasional related updates.
  • To understand how visitors use the Site so we can improve it.
  • To display relevant advertising through Google AdSense.
  • To meet our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data storage & security

Information collected through our forms is stored in Google Workspace products (Sheets, Apps Script) and Formspree, each of which applies its own industry-standard security measures. We take reasonable steps to protect the personal information we hold from misuse, loss and unauthorised access, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Data retention

We retain contact form and sign-up data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, or as required by law. You can request deletion of your information at any time — see Section 9.

8. Children's privacy

This Site is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you may request access to, or correction of, the personal information we hold about you. If you are located in the European Economic Area, you additionally have the right to request erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and to object to processing, under the GDPR.

To exercise any of these rights, email contact@blackstarmedia.com.au. If you're not satisfied with our response, Australian residents may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC).

10. International visitors

Our Site is hosted and can be accessed globally. If you access the Site from outside Australia, your information may be processed in Australia and in other countries where our service providers (such as Google) operate infrastructure.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes to our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page shows when it was last revised. Material changes will be reflected on this page.
